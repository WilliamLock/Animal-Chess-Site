鬥獸棋 Classic

A cute, polished take on the traditional animal strategy board game. Guide lions, tigers, elephants, rats, and more across rivers, traps, and dens in quick cozy matches.

Small animals, sharp tactics.

Each piece has a rank and a role. Strong animals can pressure the board, small animals can sneak through danger, and every turn asks whether to defend your den, cross the river, set a trap, or race for the rival home square.
