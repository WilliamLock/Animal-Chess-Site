Short English Post

I made 鬥獸棋 Classic because I played Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess when I was young, and wanted to bring back that childhood feeling in a friendlier way. Many versions online feel more like traditional chess apps. I wanted to make a cuter version that children can enjoy, while still keeping the classic animal ranks, rivers, traps, and dens.
