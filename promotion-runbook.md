## Main Angle

Lead with the real story:

I played Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess as a child and wanted to bring back that feeling. Many online versions feel like serious chess apps, so I made a cuter, warmer version that keeps the classic rules but feels more approachable for children and families.

## Product Hunt

Do not launch on the same day as Turbo SSHuttle or Mahjong Sudoku Classic.

Frame it as:
A cute, child-friendly version of classic Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess, made from childhood nostalgia.

Ask:
When remaking a traditional game, do you prefer classic visual style or a softer, child-friendly style?

## Indie Hackers

Use the builder angle:

I remade a childhood board game with a softer visual direction because existing versions felt too serious and chess-like.

Good question:
How do you decide when nostalgia should stay classic, and when it should become more approachable for new players?

## Reddit

Feedback wanted: a cuter version of classic Jungle Chess / Dou Shou Qi

Avoid multiple links. Use either the product home or App Store link, not both.
