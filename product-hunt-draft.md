## Short Description

鬥獸棋 Classic is a cute, child-friendly version of Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess, keeping the classic animal ranks, rivers, traps, and dens while making the game feel warmer and more approachable.

## Longer Description

I built 鬥獸棋 Classic because I played Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess when I was young, and wanted to bring back that childhood feeling.

Many versions online feel more like serious chess apps. I wanted to make a cuter version that children and families can enjoy, while still keeping the classic strategy: animal ranks, tactical captures, rivers, traps, and dens.

The game supports solo play against the computer and local two-player matches. The goal is to keep the traditional board-game logic, but make the presentation softer, friendlier, and easier to love.

## Maker Comment

Hi Product Hunt, I’m William.

I made 鬥獸棋 Classic because I played Dou Shou Qi / Jungle Chess as a child, and I wanted to bring back that feeling in a friendlier way.

When I looked at many online versions, they felt more like traditional chess apps: serious, abstract, and a little less warm. So I tried to make a cuter version that children and families could enjoy, while still keeping the classic rules.

It has animal ranks, rivers, traps, dens, solo computer play, and local two-player mode.

I’m curious how people think about remaking traditional games:

- Should the visual style stay classic and serious?
- Or can a softer, child-friendly version help new players enjoy it?
